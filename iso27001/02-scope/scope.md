# ISMS scope statement (draft)

## In scope (to validate)
- Managed IT services: infrastructure, endpoints, cloud tenants, helpdesk.
- Cybersecurity services: monitoring, vulnerability management, incident response for customers.
- VoIP services: provisioning, call routing platforms, SBCs.
- Managed print: Xerox device deployment, fleet monitoring, print management platforms.
- Physical security and surveillance: analog/PoE cameras, video management systems, physical access control, fire/burglary monitoring, lock & key.

## Out of scope (initial)
- Customer-owned environments not under a managed service contract.
- Personal devices outside approved BYOD policy.
- Legacy alarm systems slated for retirement (document list).

## Boundaries
- Physical locations: list offices, warehouses, service depots.
- Networks: corporate LAN/Wi-Fi, management networks for print and security devices, remote access paths.
- Cloud/SaaS: ticketing, RMM, MDM, print management SaaS, alarm/VMS platforms, telephony platforms.

## Interfaces and dependencies
- Vendors: Xerox, alarm/ACS/camera vendors, telecom carriers.
- TAG resources or shared tooling (if any).
- Customer data exchange channels (SFTP, portals, APIs).
